Professional Development Series

Purpose

The #BlackTech614 Professional Development Series will connect members with technology leaders in the Columbus IT industry, in an effort to share knowledge and provide guidance for career growth and advancement.

Objective

The primary objective for the Professional Development Series is to provide attendees with information and tools that will aid in further development as they continue to navigate the Columbus, IT industry.

Target Audience

The Professional Development Series will target those individuals who are interested in professional growth & development opportunities related, but not limited to, the technology industry.

Overview

Programs in the Professional Development Series will target several topics:

  • Emerging Technology
  • Creative Technology
  • Diversity & Inclusion
  • Branding & Networking
  • Technology Entrepreneurs

Learning Community Series
Purpose

The #BlackTech614 Learning Community Series empowers members to explore “in-demand” technology pathways by fostering an environment that is conducive to collaborative skill acquisition.

Objective

The primary objective for the Learning Community Series is to introduce attendees to the basics and importance of each pathway as they pertain to technology in Columbus, and to the industry as a whole.

Target Audience

The Learning Community Series will target those individuals who are interested in beginning or furthering their careers within the technology industry - specifically related to one of our 4 Pathways.

Overview

Each pathway targets an “in-demand” technology skill set, is designed to educate members technically and will increase overall awareness around areas of opportunity in the Columbus IT Market.

  • Data & Analytics
  • Design
  • Cyber Security
  • Coding
