Our story

We are dedicated to creating a safe space for Black Technology Professionals to be their authentic selves while interacting with like-minded career individuals.

By supporting #BlackTech614, you are also supporting our local companies in helping to close this gap and accelerate the progression of necessary IT initiatives with productive and profitable teams.

#BlackTech614 is building a pipeline of diverse talent with both the interpersonal and technical skills needed to keep up with the rigor of the industry.
